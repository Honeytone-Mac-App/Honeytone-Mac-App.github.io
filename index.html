What you can do with Honeytone

Text

Type text anywhere on your screen in any color and different sizes, by simple click and start typing.

Shapes

Honeytone contains expressive shapes for you to use, including fee Pen, Rectangle, Ellipse, Arrow and Lines.

Whiteboard

Toggle your screen into whiteboard or blackboard by only one click.

Colors

Honeytone provides 30 color palettes, so that you can easily choose your color.

Fading Ink

You can choose 1 seconds, 3 seconds or 5 seconds, after this time, your drawn ink will fadeout automatically after you draw it.

Eraser

Click on anything you put on screen by using eraser tool to remove them from screen easily.
